Our Story

Learn more about our organization's founding and how we came up with this initiative to help clean up the community.

Our Beginnings

Computers4change began with a simple realization. During a programming class, we—co-founders Ishaan and Krish—noticed an overwhelming amount of unused and discarded electronics gathering dust in the classroom closet. It was a stark reminder of how easily technology can become waste. Determined to make a change, we founded Computers4change to address this growing issue. Our mission started small, but with a big goal: to reduce e-waste and promote responsible recycling. Today, we continue to build a community that values sustainability, ensuring that technology can be recycled and repurposed for a better future.
